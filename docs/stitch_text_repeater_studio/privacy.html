Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Text Repeater Studio is a client-side tool. All text processing happens entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any text you enter into the tool.

We may collect limited anonymous usage data through third-party analytics services:

  • Pages visited and time spent on site
  • Browser type and device information
  • General geographic location (country/city level)

2. How We Use Information

Anonymous analytics data is used solely to understand how visitors use our site and improve the user experience. This data cannot be used to identify individual users.

3. Cookies

We may use cookies for:

  • Analytics cookies — to measure site traffic and usage patterns
  • Advertising cookies — if we serve ads through third-party networks (e.g., Google AdSense), these partners may use cookies to serve relevant ads

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics — for website analytics
  • Google AdSense — for displaying advertisements
  • Cloudflare — for hosting and CDN services

These services have their own privacy policies governing data collection.

5. Data Security

Since all text processing happens in your browser and no text data is sent to our servers, your text content remains private and secure at all times.

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@textrepeater.studio
